My Impact: Projects & Case Studies

E-commerce Conversion Booster

The Problem: A rapidly growing online fashion retailer was struggling with low on-site conversion rates and high cart abandonment despite significant traffic.

My AI Solution: I developed and deployed a sophisticated Conversational AI Agent. This agent engaged visitors with personalized product recommendations based on browsing behavior, answered queries instantly, and offered proactive assistance during the checkout process to address potential friction points.

Tangible Results:

  • 18% increase in overall conversion rate within 3 months.
  • 12% reduction in cart abandonment rate.
  • 20% uplift in average order value due to smart cross-sells.
Key Tech: NLP, Machine Learning, Recommendation Engines, E-commerce API Integration.

Autonomous Sales Lead Nurturing

The Problem: A B2B SaaS company faced inefficiencies in their sales pipeline. Leads were often followed up inconsistently, and the sales team spent too much time on manual qualification tasks.

My AI Solution: I implemented an Agentic AI Workflow that integrated with their CRM. This workflow autonomously qualified new leads based on predefined criteria, segmented them, and initiated personalized email nurturing sequences. It also scheduled demos for highly qualified leads directly into sales reps' calendars.

Tangible Results:

  • 25% increase in marketing qualified leads (MQLs) passed to sales.
  • 15% reduction in average sales cycle length.
  • 30% improvement in sales team productivity by automating routine tasks.
Key Tech: Predictive Lead Scoring, CRM Automation, AI-driven Email Personalization.
